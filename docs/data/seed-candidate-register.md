## Admission checklist

- Confirm the canonical parent company, Indian legal entity, GCC identity, and office city separately.
- Capture at least one primary company, government, filing, or reviewed public ATS source.
- Do not infer an owned GCC mandate from a generic sales office or third-party hiring listing.
- Require corroboration before assigning high confidence.
- Record canonical URL, publisher, publication/observation date, fetch date, source registry version, and evidence snippet.
- Leave the record in `draft` or `review` until a moderator approves the relevant evidence.
